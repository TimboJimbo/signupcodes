USB-C Chargers

  • Output: Look for USB-C PD (20–30W for phones, 60–100W for laptops).
  • Ports: Single vs multi-port; check total shared wattage.
  • Safety: IEC/UL listed, over-current/over-temp protection.

Power Banks

  • Capacity: 10,000–20,000 mAh for phones; higher for tablets/laptops.
  • Charging speed: USB-C PD (≥20W) and pass-through support.
  • Airline rules: ≤100Wh for carry-on travel.

Noise-Cancelling Headphones

  • ANC quality: Hybrid ANC and transparency modes.
  • Comfort: Clamp force, weight, and ear-pad material.
  • Battery life: ≥20 hours; USB-C charging preferred.

Mechanical Keyboards

  • Layout: 60%, TKL, or full-size—match your desk/workflow.
  • Switches: Tactile/linear/clicky; consider hot-swappable sockets.
  • Connectivity: Wired vs 2.4GHz/Bluetooth; multi-device switching.

Air Purifiers

  • Filter type: True HEPA (H13/H14) + activated carbon.
  • Room size: CADR appropriate for your space.
  • Noise: dB at low/sleep mode; auto mode responsiveness.
