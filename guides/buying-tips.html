What to look for (general)

  • Match specs to your real use—avoid over‑buying.
  • Size, weight, and ergonomics matter more than headline numbers.
  • Scan recent reviews for quality control and support experiences.
  • Check what’s included in the box (cables, filters, mounts).
  • Confirm warranty terms and return windows.
